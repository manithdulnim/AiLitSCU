Workshop Sneak Peek

Curious about what we'll be covering? Here is a glimpse into the topics we are exploring:

  • The Big Question: Is AI taking our jobs, or changing them? We will look at real data on emerging career trends and how you can position yourself in the AI era.
  • Agentic Coding: Discover how modern development is shifting from simple autocomplete to autonomous pipelines using tools like Claude Code, Cursor, and OpenClaw.
  • Live Demos & Builds: No boring lectures here. Watch us build a website and generate high-quality, professional images in real-time.
  • Student Challenge: Get ready to participate in a live student build session where you can suggest ideas and see them come to life!
